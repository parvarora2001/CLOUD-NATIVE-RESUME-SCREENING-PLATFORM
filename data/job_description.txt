Job Title: Senior Business Analyst – Software Development & Machine Learning

Job Description:
We are seeking an experienced Senior Business Analyst with a strong background in software development, business analytics, and machine learning. The ideal candidate will bridge the gap between technical teams and business stakeholders to drive data-driven solutions that enhance business performance. You will play a critical role in gathering requirements, analyzing complex data, and helping develop ML-powered products and software solutions.

Key Responsibilities:

Collaborate with product owners, data scientists, software engineers, and business stakeholders to understand and document business requirements.
Analyze current business processes and workflows to identify automation opportunities and data-driven improvements.
Translate business needs into detailed technical specifications for software and machine learning projects.
Support the design, development, and deployment of machine learning models by providing domain expertise and validating outputs.
Utilize business analytics tools and programming skills to analyze large datasets and generate actionable insights.
Develop dashboards, reports, and presentations to communicate findings to both technical and non-technical audiences.
Manage project scope, timelines, and deliverables while ensuring alignment with business goals.
Stay current with industry trends in software development, data analytics, and machine learning to recommend innovative solutions.
Required Skills and Qualifications:

Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Business Analytics, Data Science, or related field.
Proven experience (3+ years) in business analysis with hands-on exposure to software development projects.
Strong knowledge of programming languages such as Python, SQL, or R for data manipulation and analysis.
Experience working with machine learning models and familiarity with ML lifecycle (training, testing, deployment).
Proficiency in business intelligence tools like Tableau, Power BI, or similar platforms.
Excellent communication skills with the ability to translate complex technical concepts into business terms.
Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams.
Preferred Qualifications:

Experience with Agile/Scrum development methodologies.
Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure) and ML frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch).
Knowledge of statistical analysis and data visualization best practices.
